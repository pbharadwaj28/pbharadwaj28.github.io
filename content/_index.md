## Welcome!
I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Aerospace Engineering, specializing in experimental solid mechanics, computational design and additive manufacturing. My doctoral dissertation research focuses on the development of heterogeneous metamaterials for applications in energy absorption and impact mitigation.

## Research Interests
- **Experimental Solid Mechanics**: Understanding the dynamic behavior of engineering materials under high-strain rate loading.
- **Computational Design**: Design and discovery of novel material architectures for high-performance engineering applications.
- **Physical AI/Applied SciML**: Applying princinples of scientific machine learning to accelerate the discovery of novel mechanical responses
